# Skill: adr-writing

## Purpose
Record major technical decisions with clear context and consequences.

## ADR Structure
- Title
- Status (proposed/accepted/superseded)
- Context
- Decision
- Consequences
- Alternatives considered

## When Required
- Major dependency additions
- Architecture boundary changes
- New hosting/infrastructure patterns

## Quality Bar
- Decision is testable and specific.
- Tradeoffs are explicit (cost, complexity, risk).
