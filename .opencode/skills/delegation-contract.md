# Skill: delegation-contract

## Purpose
Provide a copy-ready delegation contract so subagents can execute without follow-up.

## Contract Template
- Goal:
- Why now:
- Scope in:
- Scope out:
- Constraints:
- Inputs:
- Files/areas to inspect:
- Definition of done:
- Required output format:

## Required Output Format
- Summary (<=10 bullets)
- Recommendation (single clear recommendation; max two options)
- Next Steps (numbered, concrete)
- Risks / Unknowns
- Confidence (1-5)
- Risk Rating (low/medium/high)
- Touched Paths
- Validation Evidence

## Quality Checks Before Delegating
- Scope boundaries are explicit.
- Validation expectations are explicit.
- Escalation triggers are called out (security/cost/destructive ambiguity).
